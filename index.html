Frequently Asked Questions

What is a senior living home?

+

Senior Living Homes or retirement homes are independent homes for seniors who want to lead a dignified life after retirement. These homes are designed with age-specific amenities.

How is senior living different from an old age home?

+

Senior living homes are designed for independent living, whereas old age homes are often institutional facilities providing basic care for the elderly.

What is the difference between a premium home and a senior living project?

+

Premium homes cater to luxury needs, while senior living projects focus on providing a safe and supportive environment with amenities for seniors.

What kind of medical assistance is available in Ashiana Senior Living?

+

Ashiana Senior Living provides on-call medical assistance, tie-ups with nearby hospitals, and 24/7 emergency services.
