Description

Employees of private companies that offer EPF benefits are eligible for gratuity after completing 5 years of service. However, certain conditions, like disability due to an accident or illness, may allow for earlier eligibility.

To calculate your potential gratuity amount, you can use a free online gratuity calculator like the one provided by Ashiana Housing. This tool helps you determine the amount you may be entitled to, based on your last drawn salary and years of service.

Gratuity Calculation Formula

The formula used to calculate gratuity is: 

                            G = n * b * 15 / 26

Where:

Variable Description
N/n Number of years of service
B/b Last drawn basic salary + Dearness Allowance (DA)
G Gratuity amount

Example of Gratuity Calculation

For instance, if you've worked for 5 years with a last drawn salary + DA of ₹30,000 per month, your eligible gratuity would be: 

                            5 * 30,000 * 15 / 26 = ₹86,538.46
Note:
  • The maximum gratuity exempt from tax is ₹20 lakh.
  • Years of service are rounded to the nearest whole number.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is gratuity?

+

Gratuity is a monetary benefit given by an employer to an employee for services rendered during the period of employment.

Who is eligible for gratuity?

+

Employees who have completed at least five years of continuous service with the same employer are eligible for gratuity.

How is gratuity calculated?

+

Gratuity is calculated as: (Monthly Salary × 15 × Years of Service) ÷ 26.

Is gratuity taxable?

+

Gratuity is tax-exempt up to a certain limit as per the Income Tax Act. Any amount exceeding this limit is taxable.
