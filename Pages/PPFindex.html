Formula Used for Calculating PPF

The formula used to calculate the maturity amount of a PPF account is: 

                      F = P * (((1+i)^n - 1) / i)

Where:

Variable Description
F Maturity amount
P Annual investment
i Interest rate per period
n Number of periods
